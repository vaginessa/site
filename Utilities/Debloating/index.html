De-bloating Windows

Pro Tip: Make a Restore Point before running these apps!

Debloating & Privacy

TronScript - The most efficient and automated way to clean, De-bloat, optimize and disinfect your old PCs, recommended.
BE SURE TO READ THE INSTRUCTIONS!

AtlasOS - A modification of Windows designed to maximize your system's performance and optimize latency.
Full Re-install required.

privacy.sexy - Easily create a custom Debloating script made for your specific needs.

PrivateZilla & ThisIsWin11 - The simplest ways to perform a quick Privacy and Security check of your Windows 10 & 11 OS.

Sophia-Script - The largest PowerShell module for Windows 10 & 11 tuning.

WPD | WindowsSpyBlocker - Tools created to intercept your Network Traffic and block Tracking requests even before they get to Microsoft.

Optimizer - Portable Utility that helps you restore your Security and increase your Privacy.

Fix-Windows-Privacy - Deletes Telemetry Registry keys that may have been skipped by other Utilities.

NTLite | MSMG Toolkit | AME Wizard - Tools to customize your Windows ISO.

Honorable Mentions

AutoRuns - See what programs are configured to startup automatically when your system boots.

BCUninstaller - A better Uninstaller than Windows's default one, really good for Bulk Uninstalling multiple apps at once.

BleachBit - A FOSS replacement for CCleaner which helps to effectively clean and uninstall apps.

Rufus | Ventoy - A tool to create bootable USB drives.
InstallWindowsWithoutUSB - A batch script to install Windows to a partition without a USB stick.

Disable hibernation to free as much space as your amount of RAM.
